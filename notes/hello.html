hello, garden

2026-06-08 — seedling

this is where thoughts grow. not polished. not final. just planted, watered, and occasionally pruned.

a digital garden is unlike a blog — it doesn't scroll chronologically into the past never to be seen again. notes get revised, cross-linked, abandoned, and revived. they're alive in the way a real garden is alive: some things bloom, some things die back, and the whole thing changes shape across seasons.

i don't know what will grow here yet. that's the point.
